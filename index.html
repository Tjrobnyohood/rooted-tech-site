SB 626 Section 144: Safe Harbor

To qualify for Safe Harbor status under Oklahoma SB 626, a business must maintain a cybersecurity program that:

  • Aligns with a recognized framework like NIST or CIS.
  • Includes administrative, technical, and physical safeguards.
  • Is updated regularly based on current threat assessments.
