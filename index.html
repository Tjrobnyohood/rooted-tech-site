SB 626 Section 144: Safe Harbor

Oklahoma Senate Bill 626 (Effective Jan 1, 2026) provides an Affirmative Defense for businesses that implement "Reasonable Safeguards." This protects your business from certain civil penalties in the event of a breach.

Official Links:

  • View Full Bill Text (LegiScan)

Safe Harbor Requirements:

  • Written cybersecurity program aligned with NIST or CIS frameworks.
  • Documented risk assessments and employee security training.
  • Incident response plans and layered technical defenses.
