ROOTED TECH SERVICES - RULES OF ENGAGEMENT (RoE)

1. PURPOSE: This document defines the boundaries for security testing 
   between Rooted Tech Services (The Auditor) and the Client.
   
2. SCOPE: Testing is limited ONLY to the IP addresses and physical 
   locations provided by the Client in the signed Audit Request.
   
3. METHODOLOGY: We follow NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) standards 
   to identify, protect, and detect vulnerabilities.
   
4. AUTHORIZATION: The Client represents that they have the legal 
   authority to authorize testing on the target infrastructure.
   
5. LIMITATIONS: No "Denial of Service" (DoS) attacks will be 
   performed unless explicitly requested. Data integrity is the 
   priority.
   
6. CONFIDENTIALITY: All findings are "Eyes Only" for the Client 
   and will be encrypted during storage and delivery.
